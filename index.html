More Info close

Elijah James De Ocampo, professionally known as ‘Prophet Eli’ is a 21 year old recording artist and producer from North London. Originally from the Philippines, Prophet grew up in a music-loving household leading him to be introduced to the violin from a very young age. Being classically trained later helped him in finding his own sound that can be described as R&B Trapsoul fused with folk-like storytelling with modern influences like The Weeknd and Bryson Tiller and also the iconic voices of Donny Hathaway and Marvin Gaye.

Prophet is currently working on a debut single to properly introduce his sound to the world. He currently has songwriting and sound-engineering credits on multiple songs both in and outside of The 42 Collective and has many more works on the way.

When asked about his career ambitions Prophet simply said, “ I just want to be able to look at my legacy and catalogue in the future knowing that I put my heart and soul into every piece of work I created.”

Get ready to hear his sweet vocal tone, extravagant, lilting melody lines and his raw story-telling globally along with the whole 42 Team...

Born and raised in north London, Tyka$h is an up and coming (American influenced) trap artist evolving his unique sound inspired by his brother, Kari Ca$h, who passed away May, 15th 2018. Growing up in a music based family, it was very hard not for him to involve himself in the music scene. At the age of 13 he released his first song ‘nandos’ on soundcloud and started the journey of building a fan base. now , at his current age of 15, he’s released 12 songs professionally and has evolved his sound drastically.

More Info close

Born and raised in North London, Joseph Urmaza better known as DRE4M is the next up and coming R&B/Trap artist on the music scene, But he's no rookie. Growing up in a music based family it was almost inevitable for the young singer to take on music and was first brought into the studios by his cousins at the early age of 10. DRE4M has always had an ear for great music listening to artists like Chance The Rapper, Bryson Tiller and Chris Brown growing up. This is where his unique sound stemmed from and was showcased in his single 'diamonds' which was released shortly after his debut EP 'Day-Dre4ming' which was the kick off to his music career. On his first release, the ambitious 13 year old was ready to start his journey to become the next biggest UK artist. Going into the future, DRE4M is exhilarated to get his music out there for his fans to listen to and can't wait to fulfil his passion for music.
